HOW TO BUY Bom Inu

  • Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

    Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that, you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

  • Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

    Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

  • Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $BOMB

    Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address (Address Will Be Updated Here After Swap Ends) to select BOMB. Set the slippage tolerance to 15% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

  • View $BOMB INU and HODL

    Swap BNB for BOMB INU. Now you need to add BOMB INU to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your $BOMB INU. Lastly HODL!
